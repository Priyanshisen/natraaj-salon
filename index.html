Meet Nirmala Sen

With over 15 years of passion and dedication, Nirmala Sen founded Natraaj Beauty Salon with a simple belief — every woman deserves to feel beautiful. What started as a dream has blossomed into a trusted beauty haven for thousands of women.

Our specialty lies in our homemade herbal products — crafted with love using traditional recipes passed down through generations. From our signature Hair Henna to our nourishing Hair Oil, every product is made with pure, natural ingredients.
